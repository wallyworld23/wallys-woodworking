How It Works

From Idea to Heirloom

A simple, transparent process so you always know where your piece stands.

01

Free Consultation

We talk through your vision, your space, your budget, and your timeline. No obligation — just an honest conversation about what's possible.

02

Design & Quote

I sketch the design and provide a detailed written quote. You'll know the exact cost, materials, and expected completion date before a single board is cut.

03

Crafting Your Piece

I build your piece in my Virginia Beach workshop, selecting each board for grain and color. Progress photos are sent at key milestones.

04

Delivery & Installation

I personally deliver and install your piece, making sure it's exactly right. Your satisfaction is guaranteed — if anything isn't perfect, I'll make it right.
