Service One

Custom Furniture

Every piece of furniture I build starts with a conversation about how you live. Your space, your style, your exact dimensions — then I design and build something made for you and no one else.

Tables, cabinets, shelving, desks, beds, benches, built-ins — if you can imagine it and it can be built from wood, I can build it.

  • Dining & kitchen tables (including live-edge and custom sizes)
  • Entertainment centers & media cabinets
  • Floating shelves & wall-mounted storage
  • Home office desks & built-in bookshelves
  • Bedroom furniture — beds, dressers, nightstands
  • Mudroom benches & entryway storage
  • Built-in cabinetry & closet systems

Starting prices vary by complexity and materials. A written quote is always provided before any work begins.

Service Two

Home Décor & Accents

Not every piece has to be a big project. I also craft smaller decorative items and kitchen accessories — perfect as gifts, housewarming presents, or to add a handcrafted touch to any room.

Each item is made with the same care and attention as my larger furniture pieces. No two are exactly alike.

  • Charcuterie & serving boards (live-edge, end-grain, standard)
  • Cutting boards — personalized and gift sets
  • Serving trays with handles
  • Candle holders & decorative accents
  • Wooden signs & custom engravings
  • Wine racks & bottle holders
  • Custom gifts for weddings, birthdays & housewarmings

Most décor items are available as ready-made or custom orders. Contact me to discuss options.

Service Three

Repairs & Restorations

Got a piece with real sentimental value that's seen better days? I restore and repair heirloom furniture and transform dated pieces into things you'll want to keep forever.

There's something deeply satisfying about taking a beat-up antique — something a grandparent left behind — and returning it to its original beauty. I take that work seriously.

  • Full refinishing — strip, sand, stain, and re-coat
  • Structural repairs — loose joints, broken legs, wobbly frames
  • Surface repairs — scratches, water rings, gouges, veneer lifting
  • Hardware replacement & upgrades
  • Antique & heirloom furniture restoration
  • Re-gluing & re-doweling loose or failed joints
  • Color matching to blend repairs seamlessly

Repair quotes are based on the piece's condition. Send me a photo and I'll give you an honest assessment.

How It Works

From Idea to Heirloom

A simple, transparent process so you always know where your piece stands.

01

Free Consultation

We talk through your vision, your space, your budget, and your timeline. No obligation — just an honest conversation about what's possible.

02

Design & Quote

I sketch the design and provide a detailed written quote. You'll know the exact cost, materials, and expected completion date before a single board is cut.

03

Crafting Your Piece

I build your piece in my Virginia Beach workshop, selecting each board for grain and color. Progress photos are sent at key milestones.

04

Delivery & Installation

I personally deliver and install your piece, making sure it's exactly right. Your satisfaction is guaranteed — if anything isn't perfect, I'll make it right.
